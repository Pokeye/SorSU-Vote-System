Help / FAQ

How do I vote?
Go to the Homepage and tap “Vote Now”, then choose a candidate and submit your vote.
I forgot my password. What should I do?
On the login screen tap “Forgot Password?”, enter your email, verify the code, then set a new password.
Why can’t I submit?
Make sure all required fields are filled in, and check your internet connection if your device is online.
Where can I see results?
Open the Leaderboards page to view tallies and rankings (if available for your organization).
